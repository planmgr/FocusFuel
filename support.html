Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a routine?

Tap the "+ Add routine" button at the bottom of the home screen. Set a name, type, and duration.

How do I set Today's Routine?

Swipe a routine card to the right and tap the ✨ button, or tap the empty card at the top of the home screen.

Why am I not getting timer notifications?

Make sure notifications are enabled for FocusFuel in your iPhone Settings → Notifications → FocusFuel.

How do I switch the language?

Tap the KR / EN toggle in the top right corner of the home screen.
