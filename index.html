Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

FocusFuel: Routine Timer ("the App") is developed by Junhan Lee. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data is stored locally on your device only.

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Routine configurations you create (name, duration, type)
  • Timer usage statistics (focus time, break time, streak count)
  • App preferences (language setting)

2. Permissions

  • Notifications: Used to alert you when a routine timer ends. Notification data is never sent to any server.
  • Background Audio: Used to play alert sounds when the timer completes.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
